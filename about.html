About Building Cost Estimator

The Building Cost Estimator is a web application designed to provide an estimate of the construction costs for a building project. By inputting relevant details such as the house area, number of floors, rooms, kitchens, and halls, the application calculates an estimation of the material quantities and costs involved in the construction process.

The estimation is based on predefined unit costs for various construction materials like bricks, cement, aggregate, sand, TMT bars, concrete, and wood. The application performs the necessary calculations and presents a breakdown of the material quantities and costs, along with the total cost for the project.

This Building Cost Estimator aims to provide users with a quick and convenient way to obtain an initial cost estimate for their construction projects. It can be used for planning purposes, budgeting, or as a reference tool during the construction process.
